How STRPY collects, uses, and protects your personal information

STRPY is committed to appreciating and respecting the choice and privacy of its user base and business associates when they use our website and services. This statement highlights and gives information about the privacy practices employed by us in relation to the personal information we hold of our business associates and users.

Definition & Description

For the purposes of this privacy policy, personal information and data include the data and information that can be identified or linked to specific users. This information may include:

  • Name, address, mailing address, and email ID
  • Telephone and mobile phone numbers
  • Debit/credit card details, cardholder name, expiration date
  • Mobile recharge, data card, and DTH service information
  • Electricity connection data and billing details
  • Any other details voluntarily provided while using STRPY services

⚠ Exception to STRPY Privacy Policy: Data and information provided by users/business associates in public forums, live chat rooms, blogs, social networking sites, discussion boards, classifieds, or community spaces are not deemed confidential and hence are not considered as personal/sensitive information under STRPY's privacy policy. Such information is not protected under this policy.

Use of Personal Information & Data

STRPY uses the personal data and information given by users/business associates to provide specific services explicitly requested. Personal information is used to:

  • Troubleshoot concerns and resolve disputes
  • Collect money and promote safe, secure services
  • Inform users and associates about our services, offers, and updates
  • Assess the interest of users and associates in our services
  • Prevent errors, criminal activity, and fraud
  • Enforce terms and conditions of service

Disclosure of Information & Data

STRPY shares sensitive or private confidential data or information with an external organization only when it is necessary to enable us to provide services to business associates and users. We may also share information when:

  • Necessary to complete a legitimate transaction
  • Required for reporting purposes under applicable terms and conditions
  • Required or permitted by applicable law

In no other scenario shall we share the confidential and personal data and information of our users and business associates.

Security Practices

STRPY undertakes necessary and reasonable precautions for maintaining the confidentiality and privacy of data and information of our users and business associates. For this purpose, we:

  • Provide restricted access to persons who may be required to assess the data and information (on a need-to-know basis)
  • Provide necessary training to employees for ensuring safe and proper handling of data
  • Employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information

Links to Other Websites

The STRPY website contains links to other websites. Users should understand that when they click on any of these links, they enter into another website. We assume no responsibility for these external websites.

We encourage users to read and understand the privacy statements of these websites, which may differ from STRPY's privacy policy. Users and associates are solely responsible for ensuring the security and secrecy of their membership accounts and passwords.

Privacy Policy Updates

STRPY has the full rights to make changes to the data privacy practices it uses/employs, and to update this privacy statement whenever a need arises. All updates and changes will be made available on our website. We encourage users to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.

Questions About Our Privacy Policy?

Our team is always ready to clarify any concern you may have about data privacy.
