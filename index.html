Goal & Strategy

Our Vision

At STRPY, our vision is to simplify digital transactions for every Indian through a secure, fast, and user-friendly platform. We aim to empower local retailers and consumers by providing seamless services like mobile recharge, utility bill payments, AEPS, broadband, insurance, and more.

Our goal is to ensure easy access to digital services in every town and village across India—making financial inclusion a reality for all.

Business Mission 01

Expanding digital reach to the last mile.

Business Mission 02

Building a secure franchise ecosystem.

To bring digital convenience to every corner of India, STRPY is building a vast franchise and distributor network across villages, towns, and cities. Each franchise operates a user-friendly terminal connected to the service provider’s servers, executing real-time transactions that are secure and equally authorized.

Through this growing network, we aim to provide a mini digital service center in every locality, ensuring financial services are available even in the most remote areas.

Frequently Asked Questions

You can start your retail banking business here by registering your ID at ASL Wallets Digital Portal. Thereafter you can start your ID here with all the necessary services.

After registering your ID at portal, for activation of all services you will need to pay for services. You have to call your sales person or given helpline number for payment details.

You can easily create your account by visiting our portal and thereafter: Register → Fill Details → Submit Details → Receive Password via SMS.

Once your ID has been created and work has been started, you will be provided complete training about our services and how to work at portal. Our team will guide you through every step.

Same procedure for distributor, to become our distributor you have to pay the required charges and thereafter contact your sales person for further details.

The commission will be distributed as per given slabs, and will automatically be credited to your ID wallet. You can withdraw it anytime from your dashboard.
