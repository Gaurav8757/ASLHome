About STRPY

India’s Leading Digital Payment & B2B Service Aggregator

STRPy is a brand under AHPS backed by investments from ICICI Bank and Kotak Bank. It was incorporated in 2016 with a vision to bring Financial and Digital inclusion through technology built around the India Stack. Since then STRPY has grown it's network to over 10,00,000 Merchants and 30+ Corporates, offering a complete suite of Payment and Onboarding products, such as, AEPS, Cash Management, BBPS, IMPS, BHIM Aadhaar Pay, UPI, MicroATM and QR. New Web has partnered with multiple Banks as a corporate Business Correspondent (BC) and as a Master Merchant, thereby creating a resilient back end infrastructure.

The need for developing simple yet powerful products targeted towards the underserved and unbanked, triggered the inception of the company. With the advent of the solution, we are now successfully able to help the rural people to uplift themselves with additional income generation, banking access, credit access, digital payments and other financial services. To further this cause we are developing a Super APP Platform for the rural / semi urban population that will enable them to seamlessly interact with various financial products and services.

Goal & Strategy

Our Vision

At STRPY, our vision is to simplify digital transactions for every Indian through a secure, fast, and user-friendly platform. We aim to empower local retailers and consumers by providing seamless services like mobile recharge, utility bill payments, AEPS, broadband, insurance, and more.

Our goal is to ensure easy access to digital services in every town and village across India—making financial inclusion a reality for all.

Business Mission 01

Expanding digital reach to the last mile.

Business Mission 02

Building a secure franchise ecosystem.

To bring digital convenience to every corner of India, STRPY is building a vast franchise and distributor network across villages, towns, and cities. Each franchise operates a user-friendly terminal connected to the service provider’s servers, executing real-time transactions that are secure and equally authorized.

Through this growing network, we aim to provide a mini digital service center in every locality, ensuring financial services are available even in the most remote areas.
